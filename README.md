### Usage

1. **Add Music**
   - Drag and drop audio files onto the upload area, or
   - Click "Browse Files" to select files from your computer

2. **Control Playback**
   - Click the play button or press `Space` to start
   - Use the control buttons or keyboard shortcuts to navigate
   - Click the progress bar to jump to any point in the song

3. **Customize**
   - Toggle between dark and light themes
   - Adjust volume to your preference
   - Enable shuffle or repeat modes as needed
